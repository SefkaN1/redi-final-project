ABOUT US

ReDI Alumni Website is an initiative of ReDI School Aarhus, which connects former students with companies looking for new employees. ReDI Alumni community reflects all the incredible journeys of women with migrant and refugee backgrounds and their accomplishments as a part of ReDI school ecosystem. Our graduates have pursued their careers in several directions, such as software developing, data analysis, UX/UI Design, cybersecurity and more, which has influenced our community to grow and expand through the years

OUR CORSES

Our courses at ReDI School Aarhus were created to give people the digital skills they need to succeed in the quickly changing world of technology today. Our wide selection of courses accommodates students with varying backgrounds and ability levels. n order to develop our community, we place a high priority on cherishing a collaborative and inclusive learning environment, cultivate problem-based learning, group projects, workshops, and networking gatherings, as we believe in collaboration and the sharing of ideas, assisting our students in acquiring critical soft skills in addition to their technical knowledge. These are the subjects we cover at our school: Data Analytics, JavaScript (I II), Cybersecurity and Machine Learning, UX/UI Design Basics
